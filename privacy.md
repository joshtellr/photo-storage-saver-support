# Privacy Policy — Photo Storage Saver

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

Photo Storage Saver is designed so your photos and videos never leave your
device — on iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal
data. There is no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party
SDKs.

## How your library is used

- Photos and videos are read **locally** through Apple's PhotoKit framework,
  only after you grant Photos permission.
- Duplicate detection (Apple's Vision framework) and video compression
  (AVFoundation) run **entirely on your device**.
- The app makes **no network calls** with your photo data.

## Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple via the App Store. We never see your
payment information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's
own privacy policy.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL
with a new date. Since the app collects nothing, changes are unlikely.
